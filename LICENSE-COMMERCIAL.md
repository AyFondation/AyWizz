This dual licensing model lets AyWizz remain genuinely open to the
community while sustaining its development. Commercial license fees

## When you need a commercial license

You need a commercial license if **all** of the following are true:

1. You intend to use AyWizz, in whole or in part, as a component of a
   product or service that you distribute or offer to third parties.
2. That product or service is closed-source, proprietary, or otherwise
   not licensed under terms compatible with AGPL v3.0.
3. You are **not** willing to release the complete corresponding source
   code of your product or service under AGPL v3.0 in accordance with
   AGPL §13 (the "remote network interaction" clause).

You do **not** need a commercial license — and you may use AyWizz freely
under AGPL v3.0 — if:

- You are an individual using AyWizz for personal, research, or
  educational purposes.
- You are an organization using AyWizz internally without redistributing
  it or offering it as a service to third parties.
- You are an organization that distributes or hosts AyWizz **and** you
  are willing to comply with all AGPL v3.0 obligations, including the
  source-disclosure requirement of AGPL §13.

## What a commercial license grants

A commercial license, negotiated case-by-case with the Licensor, grants
the licensee the right to use, modify, and redistribute AyWizz (in whole
or in part, including modifications) under terms **other than the
AGPL v3.0** — typically allowing closed-source integration into a
proprietary product or service.

The exact scope, duration, fees, and territorial reach of any commercial
license are specified in the signed commercial license agreement
between the Licensor and the licensee. No commercial license is granted
implicitly.

## How to request a commercial license

Two equivalent channels — pick whichever suits you best:

1. **GitHub issue** (preferred for first contact, public discussion of
   non-confidential aspects):

   - Open an issue on the AyWizz repository:
     <https://github.com/AyFondation/AyWizz/issues/new>
   - Apply the label `license` to the issue.
   - Describe, at a high level, your intended use, the scale (number of
     users, deployment context), and the terms you are seeking.
   - The Licensor will reply within the issue or move the discussion to
     email if it becomes confidential.

2. **Email** (preferred for confidential discussions and contract
   negotiation):

   - <ay.fondation@tuta.io>
   - Please include in your message: company / organization name,
     intended use of AyWizz, scale of deployment, and any specific
     license terms you require.
   - Expect an initial reply within a few business days.

Both channels reach the same Licensor.
